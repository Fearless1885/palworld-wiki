title: Fresh Save or Carry Over?
---
- **Start fresh if you can.** 1.0 rebuilt the main story and progression around a new tutorial flow — Pocketpair themselves recommend a new character, and mission progress resets to fit the new reward structure either way.
- If you do carry an old world over, **back up the save first and remove old mods** — stale mod files can corrupt a 1.0 save.
- World settings can be changed at any time, so don't agonise over them at creation (see the settings card below).
