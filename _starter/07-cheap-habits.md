title: Habits That Pay Off All Game
---
- **Grab every Pal Effigy** (they glow green — hunt at night on a flying mount) and turn them in at the Statue of Power to raise your Capture Power.
- **Save every weapon and armour blueprint** — duplicates included. They upgrade all the way to legendary at the Drafting Table later.
- **Drop map markers** on dungeons, dense ore fields and Skillfruit trees so you can farm them on a loop.
- Poke into the new **Ancient Ruins** minigames for free schematics, and climb **Watchtowers** — they reveal the map and double as fast-travel points.
- Sweep up **fast-travel points** as you pass: they grant Technology Points, while boss kills grant **Ancient Technology Points** for the top-tier tree.
