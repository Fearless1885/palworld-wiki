title: Your First Hour
---
- Gather wood and stone, build a **workbench, campfire and straw bed**, and craft a handful of Pal Spheres immediately.
- **Your very first sphere goes on a Cattiva** — it works your base right away, and its Cat Helper skill adds **+50 carry weight per Cattiva in your party** (it stacks).
- Catch **5 of every species** you meet — the capture XP bonus caps at five per species in 1.0 and is the fastest levelling engine in the game.
- **Do every mission that appears.** Quests were reworked in 1.0 and now gate fast travel, spawns and progression — they're not optional flavour anymore.
- Capture Alpha and dungeon bosses instead of killing them — the capture reward is bigger.
