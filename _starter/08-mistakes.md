title: Beginner Mistakes to Avoid
wide: true
---
- **Don't rush the World Tree.** It's a Level 80 endgame region behind a quest chain — a roaming boss will end an under-levelled run in seconds. Clear the Sunreach sky islands first.
- **Stay out of Wildlife Sanctuaries early** — in 1.0 they're patrolled by drones, searchlights and boss Pals. Come back geared.
- **Check the tech tree before selling materials** — recipes were reworked in 1.0, so yesterday's junk may be today's key ingredient.
- **Use the new dash.** The dodge is now a dash and you can attack and reload mid-dash — it changes how fights flow.
- **Never skip missions** — they gate progression now, not just XP.
