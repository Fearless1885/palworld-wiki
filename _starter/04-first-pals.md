title: First Pals & Mounts
---
- **Cattiva** — carry weight + base labour from minute one.
- **Foxparks** — its flamethrower partner skill melts the first (Grass) tower boss, cooks food, and keeps you warm at night.
- **Celaray** (Level 7) upgrades your glider; **Eikthyrdeer** (Level 12) is your first proper ground mount with a double jump.
- **Rush a Nitewing** — the saddle unlocks at **Level 15**, and flight is the single biggest quality-of-life jump in the early game.
- Later, the new 1.0 **Wing Pack** glider flies on Wing Cells with no Pal equipped — freeing all five party slots for a combat team.

*Full role breakdowns and passive builds for every one of these are on the Wiki tab.*
