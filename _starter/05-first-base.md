title: Your First Base
---
- Anywhere **flat and open** works at first — early raids are easy. Don't overthink placement; you'll move.
- Focus on **upgrading the Palbox to Level 10**: that grants your first base upgrade and unlocks additional bases (up to four on default settings). Level 10 is the natural moment to relocate somewhere better.
- Assign **single-skill Pals to single jobs** — work suitability now spans up to ten levels in 1.0, so a specialist on the right task massively outpaces a distracted jack-of-all-trades.
- Once you can fly, a community favourite is the **unraidable ledge near coordinates 191, -39** (by the Sealed Realm of the Guardian teleport) — ground raids simply can't reach it.
