title: Where to Put Stat Points
---
- Early on, **quality of life beats combat stats**. Prioritise three: **Weight** (fewer trips home), **Work Speed** (you out-craft your Pals for a while), and **Stamina** (sprinting, mining, gliding — it pays off constantly).
- Hold off on heavy **Attack and HP** investment until tougher content demands it.
- You can eventually max everything — permanent boosts also come from **Remedies and Elixirs** in supply drops or the Medicine Workbench — so nothing here is a permanent mistake.
