title: How the Offspring Species Is Decided
---
- Every species carries a hidden **breeding-power value**. Most pairs average the parents' values, and the child is the species closest to that average — so **the same pair always produces the same species**.
- Thousands of pairs converge on the same child: use a **reverse-lookup breeding calculator** to route from Pals you already own instead of chasing one rigid recipe.
- **Same species × same species stays that species** — that's how you refine passives and IVs after landing your target.
- **Fixed Fusion pairs override the math** (e.g. Mossanda + Grizzbolt always = Mossanda Lux), and **Legendaries only come from same-species pairs** — no combo shortcuts into a Jetragon.
- **1.0 warning:** combinations were comprehensively reviewed and strong Pals pushed later in progression — Early Access combo lists are dead. Verify pairs in an updated calculator before spending Cake.
