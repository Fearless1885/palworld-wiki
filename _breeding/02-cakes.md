title: The Cake Pipeline & 1.0's Four New Cakes
---
Build a renewable Cake line before chaining generations: **wheat plots + a mill** (flour), **red berry plots**, a ranched **Mozzarina** (milk), **Chikipi** (eggs) and **Beegarde** (honey). With those five running, the farm never idles.

1.0 added four cakes that steer the odds — match the cake to the phase:

| Cake | Effect | Use when |
|------|--------|----------|
| **Mushroom** | Slightly better stat rolls at birth | Polishing a finished line |
| **Vegetable** | Two eggs per breed | Any volume phase |
| **Deluxe Vegetable** | Higher Mutation odds + stat growth | Jackpot-hunting on a locked pair |
| **Special** | Better multi-passive inheritance | Stacking a 3–4 passive package |
