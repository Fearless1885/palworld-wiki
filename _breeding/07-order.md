Perfecting everything at once multiplies the RNG against you. The efficient sequence:

1. **Secure the species** — reverse-lookup a route from Pals you own.
2. **Assemble the passive package** — chain generations under the four-total rule, with Special Cakes.
3. **Refine IVs** — same-species breeding against high-IV partners (IV Fruits help).
4. **Run Mutation batches** — lock the pair, switch to Deluxe Vegetable Cakes, hatch volume.
5. **Awaken the keeper** — spend World Tree Radiant Gems on the finished Pal. Breeding builds the foundation; Awakening raises the ceiling.

**The classic first project:** breed **Vanwyrm + Cinnamoth → Anubis** — an elite worker-fighter available far earlier than its level suggests. Clean its passives against a 0-passive mate, stack Artisan + Work Slave + Serious + Lucky with Special Cakes, then refine IVs same-species. The same template — *route, clean, stack, refine* — builds any combat carry, with the Legend chain feeding the passive stage.
