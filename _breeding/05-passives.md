title: Passive Inheritance & the Legend Chain
---
- A child has up to **4 passive slots**, filled from the parents' combined pool with random rolls mixed in. The tested sweet spot: keep the parents' **combined desired passives at exactly four** (2/2, 1/3 or 0/4). More than four invites junk into the slots.
- Keep **"clean" 0-passive mates** of each breeding species (one per gender) to isolate good traits — **negative passives can't be removed**, only bred out against a clean partner.
- Community testing suggests putting the **rarer passive on the male** parent for slightly better odds.
- **Active skills inherit from a parent's full known pool**, not just equipped moves — keep parents low-level for a predictable child moveset.
- **The Legend chain:** Legend (+20% Atk/Def, +15% speed) starts *only* on Jetragon, Frostallion, Paladius and Necromus — it never rolls randomly. Breed the Legendary with a compatible partner, keep the Legend-carrying child, and chain toward your target species, keeping the other parent clean each generation.
- Proven stacks: **combat** — Legend + Musclehead + Ferocious + Demon God; **worker** — Artisan + Work Slave + Serious + Lucky. World Tree passives make spicy glass-cannon options — read their drawbacks first.
