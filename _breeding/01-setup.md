title: Farm, Cake, Incubator
---
- The **Breeding Farm** unlocks at Technology Level 19 (2 Tech Points; 100 Wood, 20 Stone, 50 Fiber). Assign **one male + one female** — any two species can pair — and stock the farm's chest with **Cake**: one per egg.
- Hatch eggs in an **Egg Incubator**, inspect, and keep only offspring that move your line forward.
- **Store Cake in the farm's own chest**, not general storage — otherwise hungry base workers eat your breeding fuel.
- Not enjoying the wait? The **egg incubation world setting can be set to instant** — you still do all the breeding work, you just skip the timer.
