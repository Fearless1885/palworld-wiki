**Volume wins.** There's no cap on Breeding Farms — run 3–4 in parallel, one per chain, each chest stocked deep. **Philanthropist** parents breed twice as fast, **Nocturnal** (or Dark-type) parents keep going all night, a **Babysitter** Pal at base speeds egg production *and* incubation, and **Vegetable Cakes** double egg output. A serious multi-passive line still takes hundreds of eggs — budget patience, and remember max Condensation now needs just 48 copies, so spare hatches aren't waste.

Mistakes that cost the most time:

- Using **Early Access combo lists** — 1.0 rebalanced them; verify everything.
- **Perfecting IVs before passives are stable** — passives are the harder fix; lock them first.
- **Overloading parents** past four combined desired traits.
- **Laundering contaminated lines** — after a few cycles, restarting the stage is faster.
- **Treating Awakening as a fix** — it multiplies a good Pal, it doesn't rescue a bad line.
- **Outrunning your bakery** — a stalled Cake pipeline kills a program right when the odds start paying.
