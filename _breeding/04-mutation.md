title: Mutation — The New Breeding Endgame
---
- Every bred egg now has a **small chance to be a mutated egg**: the hatch gets **higher stats plus a unique passive** on top of whatever it inherited.
- You can't force one, but you can stack odds: lock a finished pair, feed **Deluxe Vegetable Cakes**, and hatch in bulk — **volume beats one-egg-at-a-time optimisation**.
- Once a mutated keeper exists, **breed it same-species** to spread its quality through the line.
- Bonus roll worth knowing: every bred egg also has a flat **5% chance to hatch an Alpha** — nice when it happens, but Alpha status isn't heritable, so never plan around it.
