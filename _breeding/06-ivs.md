title: IV Breeding
---
- Every Pal rolls hidden **0–100 IVs for HP, Attack and Defense** — worth up to **+30% stats** at the top. IVs and passives are **separate rolls**: a perfect-passive hatch can still have mediocre stats.
- Inheritance (per large community samples): **at least one IV always comes from a parent**; beyond that, each stat is roughly **30% from a given parent, ~40% random**. High-IV parents tilt odds, never guarantee.
- The toolkit: **Ability Glasses** to inspect stock, **IV Fruits** (raid rewards) to lock a parent's roll, and **Alpha Pals** as naturally high-IV starting stock.
- Level promising hatchlings to **~30 before judging** — IV gaps are invisible at level 1 and obvious later.
