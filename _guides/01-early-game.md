title: Early Game Essentials
---
- **Catch 5 of everything.** The capture XP bonus now completes at 5 catches per species (down from 10 in Early Access) — still the fastest levelling method in the game, and 1.0's 72 new species are a huge XP well.
- **Grab Lifmunk Effigies** (green glowing statues) everywhere you go and spend them at Statues of Power to raise your capture rate.
- Build your first base near **ore nodes** — you'll need far more metal than wood or stone by level 20.
- Unlock a **flying mount** (Nitewing is usually first) as early as possible; it changes how the whole map plays.
- Keep a **feed box stocked** and plant berries on day one — hungry Pals stop working and lose sanity fast.
