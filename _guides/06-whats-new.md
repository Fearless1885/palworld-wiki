title: 1.0 Highlights
---
- **Two new regions:** the **Sunreach** sky islands (portal atop Feybreak Tower) and the **World Tree** endgame zone (behind the Panthalus quest). Both are quest-gated — no amount of levelling opens them early.
- **72 new Pals** (47 new species + 25 variants) raise the Paldeck to 287. Standouts: Astralym, Panthalus, Solenne and Renjishi — all on the Wiki tab.
- **Work suitability now goes to Level 8** — Solenne (Handiwork 8) and Renjishi (Kindling 8) redefine what a top base worker looks like.
- The **level cap is now 80**, and the new **Awakening system** (Radiant Gems from the World Tree) adds another late-game power layer.
- Pals caught in the World Tree carry the new **World Tree passive tier** (huge buffs with built-in trade-offs).
- **21 new passives** arrived with 1.0, including mutation Rainbows like Immortality and Skymarcher — all listed on the Wiki tab.
- New endgame content includes fresh tower and raid bosses — capture or hatch them for their exclusive passives before building your final team.
- Day-one balance patches are likely: treat exact percentages as provisional for a couple of weeks and trust in-game tooltips first.
