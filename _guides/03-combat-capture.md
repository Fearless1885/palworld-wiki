title: Combat & Capture
---
- **Throw spheres at a Pal's back** for a capture bonus — sneak or circle around before committing.
- Weaken with a **weaker element** or Mercy Hit gear so you don't accidentally KO rare catches.
- Bosses telegraph heavily: dodge-roll has generous invincibility, and mounted combat lets your Pal's passives boost **your** weapon damage via partner skills.
- Capture tower bosses and Alphas rather than killing when possible — Alphas carry **exclusive passives** (see the Wiki tab).
- Bring element coverage, not six copies of your strongest Pal — resistances matter more at high level.
