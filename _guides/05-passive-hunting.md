title: Getting the Passives You Want
---
- **Fishing:** catches with green sparkles are guaranteed to carry a **Rainbow-tier passive** — the most reliable Rainbow source.
- **Implants:** buy passive implants (Runner, Infinite Stamina and more) from the Arena vendor and apply them at the **Pal Surgery Table**.
- **Yakumo's partner skill** raises the odds that wild Pals you catch share Yakumo's own passives — stack it with matching passives before a catching session.
- **Dr. Brawn** can randomly reroll a Pal's IVs and passives once per day — a gamble, but a use for spare duplicates.
- **Lucky Pals** (sparkling, oversized) are the only source of the Lucky passive; **World Tree captures** (1.0) are the only source of the new World Tree tier.
- The 1.0 **Essence Condenser** route can fine-tune passives on an existing Pal — check it before starting a thousand-egg breeding project.
