title: Base Building Tips
---
- **Specialise your bases.** One crafting hub, one ore/coal farm, one breeding ranch beats three do-everything camps.
- Use the **Monitoring Stand** to lock Pals to a single job — multi-skill Pals wandering between tasks is the #1 cause of "lazy" bases.
- Build **Hot Springs** early: they restore sanity, which keeps work speed high and prevents injuries.
- Dark-type workers (Depresso, Astegon, Lyleen Noct) **work the night shift** for free — pair them with Nocturnal or Vampiric day-workers for 24-hour output.
- Stone structures don't burn — swap out wood before your Kindling Pals torch their own workplace.
