Palworld 1.0 launched on 10 July 2026 with the World Tree region and 21 new passive skills. This wiki's data files were updated on 11 July to include all of them.

## Added

- **World Tree tier (7 skills):** Demon's Hand, Dimensional Leap, God of Destruction, Hermit Sage, Sanctified Meat Shield, Twin-Edged Holy Blade, World Tree Seedbed — high-power passives with built-in trade-offs, found only on Pals caught in the World Tree.
- **Rainbow mutation passives (5):** Babysitter, Heavily Armored, Immortality, Idiosyncratic, Skymarcher.
- **Whopper** (Tier +3): Water attack +5%, Ice attack +5%, Defense +5%.
- **Night Owl** (Tier +1): works through the night but sleeps during the day.

## Changed

- **Heavyweight** promoted from Tier +2 to Tier +3.

## Notes

Day-one numbers are provisional — expect corrections as the community datamines the release. Whopper's values in particular look modest for its tier and may be revised.
