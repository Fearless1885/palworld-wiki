The wiki's Pal data has been updated for the 1.0 release, which added 72 new Pals (47 new species + 25 elemental variants) across the new Sunreach and World Tree regions.

## Added to the Pals tab

- **Astralym** — the six-winged World Tree dragon, boss-exclusive to the final tower encounter.
- **Panthalus** — the giant whale; Level 70 quest catch and a rideable flying Water mount.
- **Shaolong** — Water/Dragon partner of Sunreach tower boss Auri.
- **Solenne** — Handiwork **Lv8**, the new crafting gold standard (Dark type, works nights).
- **Renjishi** — Kindling **Lv8**, the new furnace king.
- **Leafan, Puffolt, Muffly, Dupin** — the notable utility and base picks of the new roster.

## Updated

- Blazamut Ryu is no longer called the best Kindling worker (Renjishi took the crown).
- Anubis note adjusted — still the best fighter-worker hybrid, but Solenne out-crafts it.
- Guide updated: capture XP bonus now completes at **5 catches** (was 10), max Condensation now needs **48 copies** (was 116), breeding combos were rebalanced, level cap raised to **80**, and the Awakening system added.

## Notes

Day-one data: work suitability details for several new Pals are still being confirmed by the community, and Dupin's cloning mechanic is not fully mapped yet. Expect refinements over the coming week.
