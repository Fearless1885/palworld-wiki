Four additions in one update:

## Guess that Pal!

A silhouette guessing game now lives on the front page — four choices, streak counter, and a consistent mystery shape per Pal. It automatically upgrades to real silhouette images if PNGs are added to `assets/img/silhouettes/`.

## Work Suitability tab

A third tab on the wiki covers all 12 work suitabilities with icons, what each job powers, top workers per job, and how levels work — including 1.0's raised ceiling (Level 8 specialists) and the base-wide boosts from partner skills and Condensation.

## Complete 1.0 Paldeck additions

The Pals tab now lists **all 72 new Pals** — the 47 new species and 25 elemental variants with Paldeck numbers — as a clickable checklist that feeds the search. Species elements get filled in as the community confirms them.

## Categories & architecture

A new Categories page in the nav acts as the site's hub: Pals by role and element (deep-linked into filtered wiki views via URL parameters), systems pages, and the planned Materials/Weapons/Dungeons/NPC databases with their linking rules documented.
